About DCSA

The department of Computer Science and Applications was established in academic year 1996-97 with a program DCA (Diploma in computer Applications) and BCA (Bachelor in Computer Applications). In 1998, with the approval of the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), the MCA (Master of Computer Applications) program was started with an intake of 30 students. The start of these programs was in a small building near to university bank. In 1999, the Department has got its own new building with well equipped laboratories, library, seminar hall, faculty rooms and lecture halls.

The first batch of MCA students was released in 2001 and most of them have been absorbed in the reputed IT based companies. Many BCA students of those years have also got jobs immediately after the completion of their course. Till June 2014, more than 1000 students have completed their BCA, MCA and Ph. D. degrees and many of them are placed in reputed IT and software industries. The department offers extension services to other departments of the university and conducts training programs for officers/employees. Some of research scholars who obtained Ph. D. degree from this department are well-placed in government universities, central school, and private engineering colleges as teaching faculties. The department has three air conditioned Computer Laboratories and one computer server room.
